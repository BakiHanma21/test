1. Introduction

Welcome to Matain Hanap-Serbisyo, an online platform designed for residents of Barangay Matain to search and avail services from skilled workers who are also residents of our barangay. This system is managed by Barangay Matain officials.

Your use of Matain Hanap-Serbisyo indicates that you agree to the following terms and conditions. Please read carefully before proceeding with registration.

2. Roles and Responsibilities

2.1 Users/Residents

  • You must be a legitimate resident of Barangay Matain.
  • You are responsible for the accuracy of information provided in your registration and profile.
  • Your account is for your personal use only and cannot be lent or used by others.
  • You are responsible for paying the skilled worker for their services as agreed upon between both parties.
  • You must interact with skilled workers with respect and professionalism.
  • Any communication or transaction between you and the skilled worker must comply with the law and barangay regulations.

2.2 Skilled Workers

  • You must be a legitimate resident of Barangay Matain.
  • You are responsible for the accuracy of information about your skills, experience, and work samples.
  • You must provide quality service as agreed upon with the user/resident.
  • You must interact with users/residents with respect and professionalism.
  • You must not charge more than what is stated in your profile or agreed upon with the user.

2.3 Admins (Barangay Officials)

  • Responsible for verifying the identity of users/residents and skilled workers.
  • Will serve as mediators in disputes or problems that may arise between users and skilled workers.
  • Will maintain system order and security.

4. Dispute Resolution

In case of disputes or problems between the user/resident and skilled worker:

  • The first step is direct communication or negotiation between the two parties.
  • If the problem cannot be resolved, either party may go to the Barangay Hall and report the problem to the barangay officials who are the system administrators.
  • The barangay officials will serve as mediators and help resolve the problem according to barangay regulations.
  • If necessary, barangay officials may refer the case to higher authorities such as the municipal or city government.
